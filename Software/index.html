At the end of 2020, I began my journey in the IT World. It was pretty hard for someone with NULL experience in Computer Science to start learning to code. It was a storm of information. I had to rearrange my thinking. All the knowledge and logical skills I earned made me look to the daily life situations from another prespective. Working with computers gave me a critical thought and enhanced my creativity and problem solving capability.

During this time, I learned the fundamentals of programming in C language, in a practical way. From a game, inter-communication processes or the usage of different types of algorithms, I learned the theory by implementing solutions to the projects I was making. So, maybe now I don't know how to do something but give me a problem, I will solve it.

For some people, Programming and Electronics are a way to gain money and improve life somehow. For me it is Art. A beautiful way of expression that gives us Freedom to do whatever we want.
